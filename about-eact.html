About EACT Classes

EACT (Educate All Charitable Trust) Classes is a non-profit initiative dedicated to providing free, high-quality educational content for students preparing for competitive exams. Our mission is to make education accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background. We operate as a Charitable Trust, fueled by the generous support of our community through donations and revenue from YouTube Adsense. This model allows us to offer a comprehensive range of resources completely free of charge, including in-depth Video Lectures, high-quality PDF Study Material, and Mock Tests.

Our Mission Beyond YouTube

Beyond providing free content, EACT is committed to directly supporting students in need. A significant portion of the funds we raise through donations is used to sponsor the education of many deserving students, helping them overcome financial barriers and achieve their academic goals. Your support not only helps us create content but also directly contributes to a student's future.
